About Us

Based in Pune (India), our mission is to provide best quality product to our international customers. We seek collaborative approach with our customers and work hand-in-hand to fulfil all the requirements.

Our vision is to create a healthier world by providing healthier snacking and food alternatives for our international family of customers.

We chose Banana Chips as our first product because we see it as a healthier alternative to other high Carb snacks in the market. High in potassium and Fiber and rich in multi-vitamins, Banana chips offers itself as a nutrition packed option.

Banana Chips

Banana chips are deep fried in vegetable oil. The thickness of the chips is 1 mm -1.5 mm after frying. The banana chips are crisp and pleasant in texture with rich taste. We offer banana chips in two types, Round Cut and Long Cut.

Quality

The banana chips are manufactured in our own facilities and partner manufacturers under strict control measures and hygiene. The uniformity is maintained in terms of shape, texture and taste of the chips. Any deviation from the conformance standards leads to disposal of the chips.

Packaging

The packaging is customized according to the customer requirement. The smaller packets ranging from size of 10g to 1000g (as per customer demand) are packed in bulk in corrugated boxes for the purpose of shipment.

We provide both nitrogen filled packing as well as air tight packaging. Nitrogen filled packing increases shelf life of banana chips from 60 days to 90 days.

On-Time delivery

The lead time for delivery varies with the customer location. However for incoterm FOB Mumbai port, the production and delivery lead time is 15 days for 500 kg of banana chips.

Traceability

We provide batch codes to every batch of production along with date of manufacture to have easy traceability for the quality of banana chips. Additionally we also use codes to trace the raw materials (banana and oil) used in the production. Complete care is taken for quality from raw material to packaging and delivery of our chips.
